Meet The Elephants

Location: Elephant Arena 4
Time: 1630hrs(Staurday,Monday,Wednesday,Friday)
Price: Rs.1,000/=

Our Elephant Sacutary is one of our most priced collection of animals as it houses many rescued elephants which we have nursed back to health and many tokens of appreciation and gratitude by foreign investors and donors. Therefore, it is only fair that we provide them the best possible care and provide you the opportunity to get up and close with theese Gentle Giants.

Therefore, we present to you the opportunity to get up and close with our Meet the Elephants activity!
Here you get the chance to feed our Elephants their favourite snacks which include an assortment of fruits like Watermelon, Sugarcane, Bannanas and many more.
After treating them to some snacks you can have a great photo-op with them cause our Elephants are not camera shy at all.
Also if you are lucky and decide to visit during their bathing time you will be able to help scrub our Elephants down and help our care-takers bathe them; but you can get yourself very wet as the Elephats love a good water fight.

Meet Chakku The Sea Lion

Location: Aquarium
Time: 1600hrs(Everday)
Price:Rs.2,000/=

Chakku is a Sea Lion gifted to us by the South American National Zoo in 2016 and is a very special guest in our establishment and we try our best to make his environment as comfortable as possible. As a result Chakku loves to meet new friends and show them his cool tricks and flips in his very own pool.

Therefore,come along and meet Chakku and make sure you are camera ready cause Chakku loves to pose. Here you get the chance to feed chakku some of his favourite snacks too. Which include an assortment of fish he loves to munch on as treats.Not only that Chakku has been practicing hard and can't wait to show you all of his new tricks he has learned recently which include him Hoola Hooping, playing with his favourite balls. He also has many swimming tricks he can't wait to show.

Also don't forget to ask the care takers for his special commands which when is asked from he will gladly respond. Such as, if you ask him to "Shake" he will give you his flipper to shake and even gives you a excited High-five.

More information on Meet Chakku the Sea Lion


Snakes Are Our Friends

Location: Reptile House 1
Time: 1300hrs(Everday)
Price:Rs.1,000/=

Snakes in general are considered one of the most scary and deadly animals at first glance. Due to this misconception many people end up harming and killing snakes the second they see them and a very negative mindset has been set to our younger generation as well about snakes.

This is a very toxic misconception. No pun intended due to the fact that not all snakes are venomous and if we continue killing snakes without protecting them our country would be rundown by rodents and other pesky animals and also snakes are generally very non confrontational animals who only harm humans when they feel threatened.

Therefore, we introduce our new zoo activity which is "Snakes are our friends!" where you get to get up and close with these beautiful creatures and experience them first hands. You will be able to carry our majestic pythons on your shoulders and pet them.

More information on Snakes are our friends.


Meet The Birds

Location: Bird House 3
Time: 1400hrs(Everday)
Price:Rs.1,000/=

Come join us to get an up close Meet and Greet with our favourite and most friendly Feathery friends living with us in the Dehiwala Zoo.This can be considered a very rare and fun experience cause when was the last time you got to touch and hold a majestic Owl or Hawk!

Therefore, make sure to come see us at the Bird House and talk to our care givers of the birds and watch them train the birds and do tricks with them. If you are one of the lucky two in the audience you will be able to be part of the show and be the person the birds do tricks on to show off their skills.

You will be able to touch some of the most magestic birds like Hawks, Owls, Ostriches, Flamingo's and even parrots who will repeat everything you say so make sure you don't say anything embarasing infront of them! Also don't forget to ask the care takers for some bird seeds and feed them a handful before you leave.

More information on Meet the Birds
